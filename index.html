Overview
A goal of integrating, connecting, and automating the web from one place. A tool to empower developers and users to maximise workflow productivity. Similiar to signing in with media on websites, RilvX allows you to sign in with RilvX without creating an account for every site. Manage every linked site through the RilvX portal, from account details, profile pictures, and more.
For Developers
Take away the need to develop authentication, from account creation, user information storage, to account settings. Use our simple but heavily customizable API to integrate RilvX authentication. Join our movement toward a more efficient and productive digital future.
Explore >
For Customers
With millions of web apps, it can become a hassle to go through the account creation process often, especially as our software needs continue to increase. With RilvX, create an account once, and sign in with RilvX on every site. Manage all linked sites from the RilvX platform.

RilvX: Commands
One input field to access the web. A simpler, more efficient, and quicker method of authentication. Integrate RilvX:Commands into your web app so users can use commands such as /log-in, sign-up, /edit-account, /profile-settings, and more.
